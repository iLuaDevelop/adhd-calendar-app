# ADHD Calendar App

A gamified productivity and task management application designed to help individuals with ADHD stay organized and motivated through engaging game mechanics.

## Overview

ADHD Calendar App transforms task management into an engaging experience by combining a powerful calendar and task system with RPG-style gamification. Track your tasks, earn experience points, collect pets, complete quests, and climb leaderboardsâ€”all while managing your time effectively.

## Core Features

### Productivity & Organization
- **Calendar Views**: Daily, weekly, and monthly calendar perspectives
- **Task Management**: Create, organize, and track tasks across multiple views
- **Quick Add**: Rapidly add tasks without disrupting your workflow
- **Focus Timer**: Built-in Pomodoro-style timer for focused work sessions
- **Notifications**: Reminders and alerts to keep you on track

### Gamification System
- **XP & Leveling**: Earn experience points for completing tasks and receive level-up rewards
- **Pet System**: Collect and care for virtual pets that grow with your productivity
- **Quests**: Daily and weekly challenges that provide structured goals and rewards
- **Skill Tree**: Unlock abilities and perks to enhance your productivity
- **Leaderboards**: Compete with friends or view community rankings
- **Loot Crates**: Open reward boxes for gems, currency, and exclusive items
- **Achievements & Titles**: Earn badges and special titles for accomplishments

### Customization
- **Settings**: Personalize notifications, themes, and preferences
- **Rewards Store**: Purchase cosmetics, pet items, and premium features
- **Personal Insights**: Track your productivity trends and statistics

## ADHD-Specific Design Features

The application incorporates several design principles beneficial for ADHD users:

- **Visual Feedback**: Immediate visual confirmation for completed tasks
- **Dopamine Rewards**: Gamification mechanics provide satisfying feedback loops
- **Time Awareness**: Multiple calendar views help with time blindness
- **External Accountability**: Leaderboards and social features encourage follow-through
- **Reduced Decision Fatigue**: Quick Add and default settings minimize choices
- **Progress Tracking**: Personal insights provide tangible evidence of productivity
- **Break Integration**: Focus timer includes built-in break reminders
